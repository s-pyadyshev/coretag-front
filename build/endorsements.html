Endorse these principles

The Principles are not just a reiteration of workers’ rights. They are an explicit acknowledgement that recognising rights is not enough – businesses and other stakeholders must actively work to enable workers in their operations and supply chains to meaningfully access those rights. We hope the Principles help in that work and we encourage all interested stakeholders to endorse them.
